Explore Relationship

“Don’t assume your partner knows about everything you expect in a relationship. Let him know. A relationship should be based on communication, not on assumption”

Communicate and share your thoughts, desires, wants, needs with your partner using WeLinQ. Connect with your date, lover, partner, boyfriend, girlfriend, wife or husband by showing curiosity, compassion and warmth. On WeLinQ you can get to know each other at a deeper level by having a safe space to ask those difficult and (often) awkward questions that come up in a relationship while keeping it fun.

Within the app you'll find different collections, each collection has a set of cards (aka questions) and each question falls into a different category (life, personality, relationships, confession, family & friends, desire). Based on the stage of your relationship, situation or topic you may want to address, WeLinQ has the right collection for you to reconnect with your loved one.

Understand each other through questions for each stage of the relationship:

  • Break the ice with "First Date" questions to keep the conversation going
  • Explore if you are a good match by exploring each others goals and relationship desires
  • Spice things up when the relationship needs a spark or reboot
  • Stay in touch in a long distance relationship while still keeping the closeness with questions that let you show your affection
  • When marriage is going through a tough time and you both feel like you need a reboot

Become more conscious within the relationship you are building, by taking time to keep exploring each other at different levels and ensuring open communication is always present. Get relationship advice and date ideas within WeLinQ:

  • Explore collections made by Relationship Coaches, Therapists and Experts.
  • A community where experiences can be shared and questions can be asked in a judgement-free environment.
  • You think you have an idea of a great set of questions? Make one collection from which other users can benefit

And many more new features to come so stay tuned. We aim to make any relationship more fun and stable.
