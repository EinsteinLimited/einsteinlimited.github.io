FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WeLinQ?

WeLinQ is a card game for couple in a app. User can choose from different themed card collections like - Relationship Anxiety, Sharing is Caring etc. These collections are either by WeLinQ, Experts or Users.

Can I book session with experts using WeLinQ?

No, as of now WeLinQ doesn't support any feature to book session with the experts.

Do we need to pay to open a collection?

No, all the collections are free.

Can I make a collection?

Yes! This is the power we wanted to give our users. Any user can make their own collections. These collections will also be available for other users to play. If you have an idea - go ahead and make one.

How safe is our chat on WeLinQ?

All the text messages are end-to-end encrypted. This means even WeLinQ can't read your messages.

Explore Relationship

WeLinQ is an App to help couples ask those awkward, difficult but important questions through card collections. WeLinQ wants to help couples who just have started going out or have been in a relationship for years. If you:

  • Are wondering what to talk about on your date tonight?

  • Are having difficulty bringing certain topics up in a conversation and talk openly with your partner.

  • Want an app to help boost the connection and intimacy in your relationship

  • Want to spice things up when the relationship needs a spark or reboot

  • Are in long distance relationship and are looking to stay in touch while still keeping the closeness with questions that let you show your affection

WeLinQ is for you.
